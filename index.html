Welcome to the Architects Technical Lead Group, UK.

Purpose

The Architectural Technical Lead Group (ATLG) is a forum for architectural leads to discuss technical issues, share knowledge and resources, and drive improvement in the industry.

The group is made up of 110 members in 60 member practices across the UK, across all sectors and varying in size from 5 to 1800 staff.

How to Join

The ATLG is open to all architects, architectural technologists, and building designers in the UK.

To join the ATLG, please apply at the membership form here: ATLG membership Form

Working Groups

The ATLG currently has two working groups meeting regularly to address the Building Safety Act / Building Regulations changes that came into force in October 2023.

ATLG-WG-BRCompliance Building Regulations - Compliance Working Group

Reviewing methods of addressing the requirement to Plan, Manage, and Monitor compliance with the Building Regulations.

ATLG-WG-BRCompetence Building Regulations - Competence Working Group

Reviewing methods of addressing the requirement to demonstrate competence.

ATLG-WG-FBS Future Buildings Standards 2023 - Consultation Working Group

Developing a response to the Government Future Buildings Standards consultation proposals issued in December 2023.
