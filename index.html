Purpose

The Architectural Technical Lead Group (ATLG) is a forum for architectural leads to discuss technical issues, share knowledge and resources, and drive improvement in the UK construction industry.

The group is made up of 110 members in 60 member practices across all sectors and varying in size from 5 to 2000 staff.

How to Join

The ATLG is open to all architects, architectural technologists, and building designers in the UK.

To join the ATLG, please apply at the ATLG membership form

Working Groups

The ATLG currently has three working groups meeting regularly. Two ongoing groups to address the Building Safety Act / Building Regulations changes that came into force in October 2023, and a group responding to the Government Future Buildings Standards consultation 2023.

ATLG-WG-BRCompliance Building Regulations - Compliance Working Group

Reviewing methods to meet the requirement to Plan, Manage, and Monitor compliance with the Building Regulations.

ATLG-WG-BRCompetence Building Regulations - Competence Working Group

Reviewing methods to meet the requirements for competence.

ATLG-WG-FBS Future Buildings Standards 2023 - Consultation Working Group

Developing a response to the Government Future Buildings Standards consultation proposals issued in December 2023.

All ATLG members are welcome to join any of the working groups. Groups typically report back to the main ATLG at quarterly intervals.
