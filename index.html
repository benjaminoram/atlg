Welcome

The Architectural Technical Leads Group (ATLG) is a forum for architectural leads to discuss technical issues, share knowledge and resources, and drive improvement in the UK construction industry.

The group is made up of 110 members in 60 member practices varying in size from single practitioners to 2000 staff, across all sectors and across the UK.

How to Join

The ATLG is open to all architects, architectural technologists, and building designers in the UK.

To join the ATLG, please apply using the Membership Form

Email Group

The ATLG operates a listserver / email group system for members. Emails from members to the group are distributed to other members, providing a discussion platform.

Meetings

The main ATLG group meets once a month in an online meeting open to all members, see dates below. Most meetings include a short guest presentation and Q&A, and polling of members on a technical subject in the meeting.

Meetings are held under the Chatham House rule. Notes of key points and consensus, and polling results are circulated to members following each meeting.

Working Groups

The ATLG currently has three working groups. Two ongoing groups to address the Building Safety Act / Building Regulations changes that came into force in October 2023, and a group responding to the Government Future Buildings Standards consultation 2023.

ATLG-WG-BRCompliance - BR Compliance Working Group

Reviewing methods to meet the requirement to Plan, Manage, and Monitor compliance with the Building Regulations.

ATLG-WG-BRCompetence - BR Competence Working Group

Reviewing methods to meet the requirements for competence.

ATLG-WG-FBS - Future Buildings Standards 2023 Consultation Working Group

Developing a response to the Government Future Buildings Standards consultation proposals issued in December 2023.

All ATLG members are welcome to join any of the working groups. Groups typically report back to the main ATLG at quarterly intervals.
